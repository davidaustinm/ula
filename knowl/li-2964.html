Item 4.3.5.5.e.
This matrix has eigenvectors \(\vvec_1=\twovec11\) with associated eigenvalue \(\lambda_1=4\) and \(\vvec_2=\twovec{-1}{1}\) with \(\lambda_2=-2\text{.}\) Therefore, \(A\) stretches vectors by a factor of \(4\) in the direction of \(\vvec_1\) and a factor of \(2\) in the direction of \(\vvec_1\) before reflecting in the line defined by \(\vvec_1\text{.}\)
