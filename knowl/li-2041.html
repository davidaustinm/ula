Item f.
To convert from \(YC_bC_r\) to \(RGB\text{,}\) we multiply by \(P_{\bcal}\) so that
  1. \(\displaystyle \threevec{R}{G}{B} = \threevec{128}{128}{128}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \threevec{R}{G}{B} = \threevec{128}{107.4}{234.3}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \threevec{R}{G}{B} = \threevec{212.1}{85.1}{128}\)
