  1. Since \(Q^TQ=I\text{,}\) we have \(\det(Q^TQ)=\det(Q^T)\det(Q)=(\det(Q))^2 = \det(I) = 1\)
  2. The columns of \(Q\) form an orthonormal basis for \(W\text{,}\) a 4-dimensional subspace of \(\real^8\text{.}\) Therefore, \(QQ^T\) projects vectors orthogonally onto \(W\) so \(\col(QQ^T)=W\) and \(\rank(QQ^T)=4\text{.}\)
  3. If \(\bvec^\perp=\bvec-\bhat\text{,}\) then \(\bvec-\bperp = \bhat\) is in \(W\text{,}\) the orthogonal complement of \(W^\perp\text{.}\) This means that \(\bperp\) is the orthogonal projection of \(\bvec\) onto \(W^\perp\text{.}\)
