Item 3.2.5.5.a.
By forming the matrix \(P_{\bcal}=\left[\begin{array}{rrr} \vvec_1 \amp \vvec_2 \amp \vvec_3 \end{array}\right]\) and finding its reduced row echelon form, we see that the vectors are linearly independent and span \(\real^3\text{.}\) They therefore form a basis of \(\real^3\text{.}\)
