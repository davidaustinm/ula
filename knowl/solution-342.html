Solution 6.4.4.8.1.

  1. The entries of \(A^TA\) are the dot products \(\avec_i\cdot\avec_j\text{.}\)
  2. The entries of \(R^TR\) are the dot products \(\rvec_i\cdot\rvec_j\text{.}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle A^TA = (QR)^T(QR) = R^TQ^TQR = R^TIR = R^TR\)
  4. This follows from the previous parts of this exercise.
