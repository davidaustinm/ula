Since the columns of \(A\) are linearly independent, the only solution to the homogeneous equation \(A\xvec=\zerovec\) is \(\xvec=\zerovec\text{.}\) Since that \(A(B-C) = 0\text{,}\) every column \(\xvec\) of \(B-C\) satisfies \(A\xvec = \zerovec\text{.}\) Therefore, every column of \(B-C\) is the zero vector, which implies that \(B=C\text{.}\)
