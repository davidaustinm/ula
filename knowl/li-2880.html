Item f.
We can write \(A=PDP^{-1}\) where \(D=\mattwo{0.5}00{0.1}\text{.}\) Therefore, \(A^k=PD^kP^{-1}\) where \(D^k=\mattwo{0.5^k}00{0.1^k}\text{.}\) As \(k\) becomes very large, \(0.5^k\) and \(0.1^k\) become very close to zero. Hence \(D^k\) and \(A^k\) become very close to the zero matrix.
