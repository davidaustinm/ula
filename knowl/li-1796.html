Item 3.1.5.12.b.
Since the only solution to the homogeneous equation \(B\xvec = \zerovec\) is \(\xvec=\zerovec\text{,}\) we know that the columns of \(B\) are linearly independent and so \(B\) has a pivot position in every column. Since \(B\) is a square matrix, this tells us that \(B\sim I\) so that \(B\) is invertible.
