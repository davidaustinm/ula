We have the equation \(A\xhat = U_rU_r^T\bvec\text{,}\) which gives
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} A\xhat \amp = U_rU_r^T\bvec \\ U_r\Sigma_rV_r^T\xhat \amp = U_rU_r^T\bvec \\ \Sigma_rV_r^T\xhat \amp = U_r^T\bvec \\ V_r^T\xhat \amp = \Sigma_r^{-1}U_r^T\bvec \\ \xhat \amp = V_r\Sigma_r^{-1}U_r^T\bvec \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
