Item 3.2.5.9.e.
If \(\coords{\xvec}{\bcal} = \twovec{c_1}{c_2}\text{,}\) then \(\xvec=c_1\vvec_1+c_2\vvec_2\) and \(A\xvec=3c_1\vvec_1+c_2\vvec_2\text{.}\) Therefore, \(D\twovec{c_1}{c_2} = \twovec{3c_1}{c_2}\text{,}\) which says that \(D= \left[\begin{array}{rr} 3 \amp 0 \\ 0 \amp 1 \\ \end{array}\right] \text{.}\)
