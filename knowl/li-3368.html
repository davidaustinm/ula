Item 4.5.5.1.c.
Every two-dimensional vector is an eigenvector with eigenvalue \(\lambda=1\) so that \(A\xvec=\xvec\) for every \(\xvec\text{.}\) This shows that any Markov chain will remain constant. The steady-state vectors are those for which \(\qvec=\twovec{p}{1-p}\) for some \(p\) satisfying \(0\leq p\leq 1\text{.}\)
