Item 7.2.4.10.a.
\begin{align*} f_x \amp {}={} 4x^3 - 4yz = 0\\ f_y \amp {}={} 4y^3 - 4xz = 0\\ f_z \amp {}={} 4z^3 - 4xy = 0 \end{align*}
and all three equations are satisfied at the point \((-1,1,-1)\text{.}\)
The Hessian matrix at that point is \(H=\begin{bmatrix} 12 \amp 4 \amp -4 \\ 4 \amp 12 \amp 4 \\ -4 \amp 4 \amp 12 \end{bmatrix} \)
