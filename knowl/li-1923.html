Item 3.2.5.5.c.
We have
\begin{equation*} P_{\bcal}= \left[\begin{array}{rrr} 1 \amp \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \amp \frac{1}{2} \\ 1 \amp 0 \amp -1 \\ 1 \amp -\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \amp \frac{1}{2} \end{array}\right],~~~ P_{\bcal}^{-1}= \left[\begin{array}{rrr} \frac{1}{3} \amp \frac{1}{3} \amp \frac{1}{3} \\ \frac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \amp 0 \amp -\frac{\sqrt{3}}{3} \\ \frac{1}{3} \amp -\frac{2}{3} \amp \frac{1}{3} \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}
Since we have \(\coords{\xvec}{\bcal}=C_{\bcal}^{-1}\xvec\text{,}\) we have \(c_1=\frac13 x_1 + \frac13 x_2 + \frac13 x_3 = \frac13(x_1+x_2+x_3) \text{.}\)
