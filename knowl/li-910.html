Item 2.3.4.1.a.
The equation \(\left[\begin{array}{rr} \vvec_1 \amp \vvec_2 \end{array}\right] ~\xvec = \bvec\) is consistent so \(\bvec\) is in \(\laspan{\vvec_1,\vvec_2}\text{.}\) Since \(\left[\begin{array}{rr} \vvec_1 \amp \vvec_2 \end{array}\right]\) has a pivot position in every row, \(\laspan{\vvec_1,\vvec_2}\) is \(\real^2\text{.}\)
