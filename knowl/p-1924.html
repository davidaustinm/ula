Beginning with an initial state \(\xvec_0\text{,}\) we would like to know how the state evolves over time. For instance,
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \xvec_1 \amp = T(\xvec_0)=A\xvec_0 \\ \xvec_2 \amp = T(\xvec_1) = (T\circ T)(\xvec_0)= A^2\xvec_0 \\ \xvec_3 \amp = T(\xvec_2) = A^3\xvec_0 \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
and so on.
