Item 4.5.5.8.c.
We have
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} (A-I)\xvec \amp = \evec_1 \\ S(A-I)\xvec \amp = S\evec_1 \\ \begin{bmatrix} 0 \end{bmatrix} \amp = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \end{bmatrix}. \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
This says that the original equation \((A-I)\xvec = \evec_1\) is not consistent.
