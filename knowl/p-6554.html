Suppose we have the \(QR\) factorization \(A=QR\text{,}\) where \(A\) is a \(7\times 4\) matrix.
  1. What is the shape of the product \(QQ^T\text{?}\) Explain the significance of this product.
  2. What is the shape of the product \(Q^TQ\text{?}\) Explain the significance of this product.
  3. What is the shape of the matrix \(R\text{?}\)
  4. If \(R\) is a diagonal matrix, what can you say about the columns of \(A\text{?}\)
