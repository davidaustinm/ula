If we have an orthogonal basis \(\wvec_1,\wvec_2,\ldots,\wvec_n\) for \(W\text{,}\) then \(\bhat = c_1\wvec_1+c_w\wvec_2+\cdots+c_n\wvec_n\text{.}\) Therefore,
\begin{align*} (\bvec-\bhat)\cdot\wvec_i \amp = 0\\ \bvec\cdot\wvec_i \amp = \bhat\cdot\wvec_i\\ \bvec\cdot\wvec_i \amp = (c_1\wvec_1+c_2\wvec_2+\cdots + c_n\wvec_n)\cdot\wvec_i\\ \bvec\cdot\wvec_i \amp = c_i\wvec_i\cdot\wvec_i\\ c_i \amp = \frac{\bvec\cdot\wvec_i}{\wvec_i\cdot\wvec_i}. \end{align*}
