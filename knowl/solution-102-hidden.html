1. We first find weights \(c\) and \(d\) such that \(\twovec{1}{0} = c\twovec{1}{1} + d\twovec{-1}{1}\) by constructing the augmented matrix
    \begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{rrr} 1 \amp -1 \amp 1 \\ 1 \amp 1 \amp 0 \end{array}\right] \sim \left[\begin{array}{rrr} 1 \amp 0 \amp \frac{1}{2} \\ 0 \amp 1 \amp -\frac{1}{2} \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}
    This shows that \(\twovec{1}{0} = \frac12\twovec{1}{1} - \frac12\twovec{-1}{1}\text{.}\) Therefore,
    \begin{equation*} T\left(\twovec{1}{0}\right) = A\twovec10 = \frac12 A\twovec11 - \frac12A\twovec{-1}{1}= \twovec{1}{-2}\text{.} \end{equation*}
  2. In the same way, we find that
    \begin{equation*} T\left(\twovec{0}{1}\right) = \frac12A\twovec{1}{1} + \frac12A\twovec{-1}{1} = \twovec{2}{0}\text{.} \end{equation*}
    This gives the matrix \(A=\left[\begin{array}{rr} 1 \amp 2 \\ -2 \amp 0 \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.}\)
  3. Then \(T\left(\twovec{4}{-5}\right) = A\twovec{4}{-5} = \twovec{-6}{-8}\text{.}\)
