1. \(A\vvec_1 = \twovec{5}{2} = \vvec_1\) and \(A\vvec_2 = \twovec{-0.3}{0.3} = 0.3\vvec_2\text{.}\)
  2. We have
    \begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rl} \xvec_2=A\xvec_1 = \amp A(c_1\vvec_1+c_2\vvec_2) \\ =\amp c_1A\vvec_1 + c_2A\vvec_2 = c_1\vvec_1 +0.3c_2\vvec_2\text{.} \end{array} \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rl} \xvec_3=A\xvec_2=\amp A(c_1\vvec_1+0.3c_2\vvec_2) \\ = \amp c_1A\vvec_1 +0.3c_2A\vvec_2 = c_1\vvec_1 +0.3^2c_2\vvec_2\text{.} \end{array} \end{equation*}
    and so on.
  4. We find that
    \begin{equation*} \xvec_1=\twovec{500}{500} = \frac{1000}{7}\vvec_1 + \frac{1500}{7}\vvec_2\text{.} \end{equation*}
  5. Then
    \begin{equation*} \begin{array}{l} \xvec_2=\frac{1000}{7}\vvec_1 + 0.3\frac{1500}{7}\vvec{2}, \\ \xvec_3=\frac{1000}{7}\vvec_1 + 0.3^2\frac{1500}{7}\vvec{2}, \\ \xvec_4=\frac{1000}{7}\vvec_1 + 0.3^3\frac{1500}{7}\vvec{2}\text{.} \end{array} \end{equation*}
  6. After a very long time, the second term becomes increasingly close to zero and so
    \begin{equation*} \xvec\approx\frac{1000}{7}\vvec_1 = \twovec{5000/7}{2000/7} = \twovec{714.3}{285.7}\text{.} \end{equation*}
