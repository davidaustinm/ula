  1. What is the shape of the matrix \(A\text{?}\)

    \begin{equation*} A\twovec{1}{0}, ~~~A\twovec{2}{3}, ~~~A\twovec{0}{-3}\text{.} \end{equation*}

  4. Find all vectors \(\xvec\) such that \(A\xvec = \zerovec\text{.}\)
