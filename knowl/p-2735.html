Forming the matrix \(P_{\bcal}\) whose columns are the basis vectors, we can convert between coordinate systems:
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} x \amp {}={} P_{\bcal}\coords{\xvec}{\bcal}, \\ P_{\bcal}^{-1} x \amp {}={} \coords{\xvec}{\bcal}. \\ \end{aligned}\text{.} \end{equation*}
