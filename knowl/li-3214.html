Item 4.4.5.7.b.
The characteristic equation is
\begin{equation*} \det(A-\lambda I) = (1-\lambda)(-\lambda)-1 = \lambda^2-\lambda-1 = 0\text{.} \end{equation*}
Applying the quadratic formula, we see that the two eigenvalues are \(\lambda_1=\frac{1+\sqrt5}{2}\) and \(\lambda_2=\frac{1-\sqrt{5}}2\text{.}\)
We find the eigenvectors by row reducing the matrices \(A-\lambda_jI\text{.}\)
