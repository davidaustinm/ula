We will now explain the relationship between the previous two solution spaces. Suppose that \(\xvec_h\) is a solution to the homogeneous equation; that is \(A\xvec_h=\zerovec\text{.}\) Suppose also that \(\xvec_p\) is a solution to the equation \(A\xvec = \bvec\text{;}\) that is, \(A\xvec_p=\bvec\text{.}\)
