Matrix Multiplication

The diagram below demonstrates the effect of a matrix transformation $T$ on the plane. You may modify the matrix $A=\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & c \\ \end{bmatrix}$ defining $T$ through the sliders at the top.

Since a matrix transformation takes a vector as input and produces a vector as output, we will show the inputs and outputs on separate sets of axes. In particular, the axes on the left represent the inputs while the axes on the right illustrate how input features are transformed by $T$.
